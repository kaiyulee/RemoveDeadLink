Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

Data Usage

  • Access to bookmarks is used solely to read and remove your bookmarks upon your request.
  • Network requests are performed to the bookmarked URLs and public RDAP/DNS endpoints to determine link availability and domain status.
  • No data leaves your machine except the necessary checks to the target sites and RDAP/DNS services.

Permissions

  • bookmarks: scan and delete bookmarks
  • storage: store scan results and user settings
  • <all_urls>: request the bookmarked sites to verify reachability

For inquiries, please contact the developer.
